Today

One list. This day. Nothing else.

What it is

Today is a simple task app with one idea: what matters right now? You add a few tasks, finish what you can, and whatever’s left rolls into tomorrow. No accounts, no sync, no clutter. Everything stays on your device.

Features

  • One list for today — Add, complete, and reorder tasks. No folders or priority levels.
  • Rollover — Incomplete tasks move to the next day so you’re not carrying them in your head.
  • Calendar — Month view with completion dots; tap a day to see its tasks.
  • Analytics — Completion stats for today, this month, and all time.
  • Optional reminders — Gentle nudge for your first incomplete task. Turn them off anytime.

Download

Today is available on the App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android). Get it and start with a single list for today.
