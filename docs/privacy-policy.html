Privacy Policy

Today (“the app”) is designed to keep your data on your device. This policy describes what the app does with your information.

Data storage

All tasks, completion history, and settings are stored only on your device using local storage (e.g. SQLite). We do not operate servers that collect or store your task data. There is no account or sign-in.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app uses your device’s notification system to send optional reminders (e.g. for your first incomplete task). Notification preferences are stored locally. You can turn reminders off at any time in Settings.

Third parties

The app does not send your tasks or personal data to third-party analytics or advertising services. We do not sell your data.

App stores

Downloading the app from the App Store or Google Play is subject to Apple’s and Google’s respective terms and privacy policies. In-app purchases and store account data are handled by those platforms, not by us.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will be available at this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the app, you can open an issue or reach out via the app’s GitHub repository or listing page.
